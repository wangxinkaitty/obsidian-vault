# Functional Connectivity of the Dorsal and Ventral Attention Network and Its Role in Attentional Disengagement

## Citation

Coll, S. Y., Marti, E., Doganci, N., & Ptak, R. (2025). Functional Connectivity of the Dorsal and Ventral Attention Network and Its Role in Attentional Disengagement. _Brain and Behavior_, _15_(10), e70868. [https://doi.org/10.1002/brb3.70868](https://doi.org/10.1002/brb3.70868)

[Open in Zotero](zotero://select/library/items/PUL23DHL)

## Abstract

Resting‐state connectivity within the ventral and dorsal attention network, particularly in the right hemisphere, predicts individual differences in attentional disengagement efficiency, highlighting the role of intrinsic brain dynamics in shaping attentional control.
